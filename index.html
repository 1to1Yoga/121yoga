Looking for more FLEXIBILITY? Experiencing SORE BACK? Love practicing in a beautiful OUTDOORS? Let us help you find BALANCE and HARMONY at your own pace and foster HEALING within. OUTDOOR GROUP SESSIONS

From time to time we will run six week Group Yoga practice in the beautiful outdoors, overlooking the beautiful Brisbane River with the sound of the waves. This gentle practice is design to ease you into world of yoga to start you on your journey. Our classes kept small to ensure that you get the individual attention you need and plenty of opportunities to ask questions as you go along.

If interested in Group Classes, please confirm with us for the next start date, Captain Burke Park (under Story Bridge, see the map below). Please bring your mat, water bottle, $20 and a curious mind. We look forward to meeting you!
